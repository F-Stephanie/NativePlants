St. Louis Native Plants

This is a non-exhaustive gallery of some popular St. Louis native plants with some information about them. Except for some of the edible plants, the plants in this gallery are top performing for landscaping according to the Missouri Botanical Garden. Their metrics include having at least three seasons of interest, a long life expectancy, and availability from nurseries.
